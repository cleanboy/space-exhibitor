Cella Energy

Booth: #8033

Cella’s unique high hydrogen content materials are the most effective shield against the high energy component of space radiation, both from galactic cosmic rays (GCR) and from the sun, or solar particle events (SPE). Above a certain amount of energy, all particles, electrons and protons as well as the high-Z can cause secondary radiation to such an extent that you can actually get an increase in dose behind a metal shield. These spallation reactions and any number of particle multiplication reactions are significantly reduced by Cella’s materials.

Hydrogen stops or slows the incoming radiation by moderation as well as ionization and there are several NASA reports showing that it is more effective for a given areal density than any other material. Cella’s unique materials store the hydrogen in a lightweight tractable form. 

Cella’s technology can prolong the operational life of space satellites by several years, reduce your insurance premiums and keep astronauts alive on long endurance space missions.

http://www.cellaenergy.com/
